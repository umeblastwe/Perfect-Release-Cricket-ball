Reference Guide

What does a
good release look like?

These thresholds are drawn from biomechanics research on elite pace bowlers (90+ km/h) and coaching guidelines from Cricket Australia and the ECB. Use them to interpret your scores. Green = elite range · Yellow = acceptable · Red = needs work.
